# Authors and archivists wanted

Many links and forum attachments from the Minecraft 1.1 era have disappeared. Some author names and redistribution terms are still unknown.

We would especially appreciate help with:

- Identifying the Minecraft 1.1-era Bat mod mentioned by the original series.
- Confirming original authors and source pages for each recovered mod.
- Locating archived license or modpack-permission statements.
- Correcting mistaken credits, versions, or historical notes.
- Identifying the remaining animals hidden behind the expired collection link.
- Contacting original creators who want to clarify permissions or request changes.

## Useful evidence

Strong evidence includes an archived original forum post, the author's own website, a source repository, a readme included in an original download, or a contemporaneous video description.

Please do not upload random executable files or repackaged mod jars. Link to the historical source and explain why it is relevant.

## Corrections and removal requests

Open an **Author or credit correction** issue and provide as much supporting information as possible. A creator or rights holder may also use that form to request a correction, attribution change, link update, or removal review.

We will treat good-faith corrections respectfully and keep a visible record of important attribution changes.
